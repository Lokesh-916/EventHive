Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite my team?

Navigate to your Organization Settings > Team Members. Click on "Invite Member" and enter their email address. They will receive an invitation link to create their account and join your workspace.

Can I export event data?

Yes! You can export usage reports, task logs, and volunteer hours as CSV or PDF files from the Reports tab in your dashboard.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

EventHive has an offline mode for the mobile app. Task updates are saved locally and automatically synced once your connection is restored.
