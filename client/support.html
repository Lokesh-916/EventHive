    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I invite my team?

    Navigate to Organization Settings → Team Members. Click "Invite Member" and enter their email. They'll receive an invitation link to join your workspace.

    Can I export event data?

    Yes — export usage reports, task logs, and volunteer hours as CSV or PDF from the Reports tab in your dashboard.

    What happens if I lose internet connection?

    EventHive has an offline mode for the mobile app. Task updates are saved locally and automatically synced once your connection is restored.

    How do I contact a volunteer or organizer?

    Use the built-in chat feature available on your dashboard. Direct messages and event group chats are supported for all roles.

    Still need help?
